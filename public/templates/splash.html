Around the World in 80 Clicks is a point and click adventure game created by Jules, Antonio and Chetan in 2016. Team Phoenix based the game on Google Maps and invite you to circumnavigate the world in 80 clicks. Unlike Phileas Fogg who lost his 20,000 pounds by 5 minutes (wager equal to about £2 million in 2016). Set by friends at the “GA”, this is the Team Phoenix most acclaimed works.
