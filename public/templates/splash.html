Around the World in Eighty Clicks is a classic adventure app created by Jules , Antonio and Chetan in 2017. In the app, Team Phoenix of London attempt's to make you play this game using google Maps to circumnavigate the world in 80 clicks unlike Phileas Fogg who lost his 20,000 pounds by 5 minutes (wager equal to about £2 million in 2017). Set by friends at the “GA”,this is Team Phoenix most acclaimed works.
